Wider Wong: Portion Control

Wider Wong has a really BIG problem! He just doesn't know when to stop eating!
  • Underneath, you will see the "Calories Allowed" magic number appear.
  • By clicking on each piece of sushi below Wider Wong, you will be adding to Wider Wong's calorie count for the round.
  • For each round, each piece of sushi will maintain the same calorie amount per click.
  • Be a good friend and try to feed Wider Wong EXACTLY the number of calories as his "Calories Allowed" magic number to win.
  • Any calories short of the magic number will keep Wider Wong sad. Eating too many calories will get him sick.
